Welcome to my homepage!

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Radiology & Imaging Sciences, and the Wallece H. Coulter Department of Biomedical Engineering, with secondary appointments in the Department of Biomedical Informatics, and the Department of Medicine at Emory University & Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, GA, USA. I am a member of the Emory Empathetic AI for Health Institute at Emory University.

Research Interests

My research interests include:

  • Signal, Image, and Video Processing

  • Computaional Imaging

  • Medical Imaging

  • Inverse Problems

  • Pattern Recognition & Machine Learning

  • Image Analysis & Computer Vision

  • Signal Detection & Estimation Theory

My research interests lie in the broad area of image science with applications to various imaging technologies. I am interested in developing new multidimensional signal processing methodologies/tools and applying these tools to a wide range of imaging applications in biology, medicine, consumer electronics, homeland security, and data science. My recent focus has been on problems involving mathematical modeling of machine learning, signal transformation & representations, and information processing.
